Make Your Mark is an online sketchbook for performers and non-performers alike to connect through the act of collaborative mark-making, generated through movement.

In the current world where physical human interaction is restricted and stage doors are held shut, Make Your Mark aims to prevent physical disconnection by offering a playful digital alternative to on-site movement assemblies.
